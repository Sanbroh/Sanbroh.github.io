About Me

I am an incoming MASc student in the Systems Control group at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Lacra Pavel.

I graduated from Queen's University, Kingston in 2025 with a BASc in Mathematics and Engineering, with an option in Systems and Robotics (M11). My research interest is in the area of multi-agent control and reinforcement learning, where heavy mathematics help solve real world problems. Before graduate school, I interned at RBC Capital Markets as an algo product manager for its electronic trading platform and assisted in nuclear materials research at RMTL (Queen's). Outside of work and school, I am focused on giving back through mentorship and leadership as part of my commitment to help others around me. You can check out some of my past work and organizations here.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading books, playing board games, and searching for the best truffle fries there is!
